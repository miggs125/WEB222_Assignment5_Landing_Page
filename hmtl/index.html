WELCOME TO A WORLD OF ADVENTURE

Rock Climbing


Introduction to Outdoor Rock Climbing

This introductory class will focus on getting you accustomed to climbing on real rock. Learn how to inspect rock, practice route finding skills, determine and mitigate risk factors, gear clean-up and gear inspection.

Traditional Climbing

Traditional climbing, as opposed to sport climbing, has no bolts fixed on the wall. climbers carry their own gear made up of slings, steel wedge nuts and camming devices that are placed inside cracks.

Highline

Introduction to highline

This course will certainly get your adrenaline going! This is a more advanced course for people who have previous slackline and/or long-line experience and are looking to take their slacklining to the next level.
